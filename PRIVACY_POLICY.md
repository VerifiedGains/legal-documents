# InvestingApps Portfolios Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 3, 2026**

## Introduction

InvestingApps Portfolios ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Reddit application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Reddit Account Information**: Your Reddit username and public profile information (as permitted by Reddit's API)
- **Brokerage Connection**: When you connect a brokerage account, we receive read-only access to your portfolio data through SnapTrade

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App (features used, pages viewed)
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system

### Information from Third Parties
- **Reddit**: Profile and authentication data
- **SnapTrade**: Portfolio holdings, account balances, and trading activity (read-only)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Display your verified portfolio holdings and trading history
- Show your performance on community leaderboards
- Assign "Verified Investor" flair to your Reddit account
- Improve and maintain the App
- Respond to your inquiries

## Information Sharing

### Public Information
By using this App, you understand and agree that:
- Your portfolio holdings are publicly visible to other Reddit users
- Your trading history is publicly visible to other Reddit users
- Your position on the leaderboard is publicly visible

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### Third-Party Services
The App integrates with third-party services with their own privacy policies:
- **Reddit:** [Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **SnapTrade:** [SnapTrade Privacy Policy](https://snaptrade.com/privacy-policy)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Data is stored on Reddit's Devvit platform (Redis) in the United States
- **Security**: We implement reasonable security measures including:
  - Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  - OAuth-based authentication (no passwords stored)
  - Read-only brokerage access

## Data Retention

- Active Users: We retain your data while you actively use the App
- Inactive Users: Data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- Deletion Requests: You can request data deletion at any time 
- Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law


## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use:
- **Local Storage:** To save preferences and session data in your browser
- **Cookies:** For authentication and user experience (managed by Reddit/Devvit)
- **Analytics:** To track usage patterns and improve the App

## Your Privacy Rights

You can:
- View your stored data within the App
- Disconnect your brokerage account at any time, which removes portfolio data
- Request deletion of your data

### California Residents (CCPA)
You have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### European Residents (GDPR)
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data
- Data portability
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority


## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
